Understanding Dandruff

What Causes Dandruff?

Dandruff is a common scalp condition that affects 20% of Singaporeans. Understanding the underlying causes of dandruff is essential for effective management.

Seborrhoeic Dermatitis

A leading cause of dandruff, this condition results in oily, irritated skin and is often associated with the yeast Malassezia.

Dry Scalp

Lack of moisture can lead to flaking, especially in air-conditioned environments common in Singapore.

Sensitivity to Hair Products

Certain shampoos or hair treatments may irritate the scalp, triggering dandruff-like symptoms.

Malassezia Overgrowth

This naturally occurring scalp fungus can grow excessively in oily conditions, causing irritation and visible flakes.

Psoriasis

A chronic condition that leads to thick, scaly patches on the scalp, which may flake off and resemble dandruff.

Stress and Hormonal Changes

High stress levels or hormonal imbalances can disrupt the scalp's natural balance, leading to dandruff.
