Frequently asked questions.

Here are the things new clients ask us most about our oily scalp treatments.

Why is my scalp so oily even when I wash it daily?

Frequent washing can actually make oily scalp worse! When you over-wash, your scalp compensates by producing even more oil. The root cause is often overactive sebaceous glands, hormonal imbalances, or product buildup. Our AI-powered diagnosis identifies your specific trigger to create an effective, lasting solution that breaks this cycle.

How is DrScalp different from using clarifying shampoos?

Clarifying shampoos provide temporary relief by stripping oils, which often triggers rebound oil production. You'll see visible improvement from your very first session! Most clients notice reduced oiliness and fresher-feeling hair immediately after treatment. As you continue your personalised treatment plan over weeks 2-6, oil production normalises progressively. By weeks 7-8, you'll experience the full WOW transformation - a balanced scalp with hair that stays fresh longer.

No pain and zero downtime. Our NanoJetâ„¢ system is completely needle-free, delivering treatments through gentle pressure. Most clients describe a refreshing, cooling sensation during treatment. You can return to work or daily activities immediately after your session.

Can oily scalp cause hair loss?

Yes, if left untreated. Excess oil can clog hair follicles, trap dirt and bacteria, and cause inflammation that weakens hair roots over time. This can lead to hair thinning and increased shedding. Our treatments address oily scalp before it progresses to hair loss issues.

What happens during my first appointment?

Your first visit includes: (1) AI-powered scalp analysis, (2) comprehensive consultation with our consultants, (3) unbiased personalised treatment plan, (4) detailed explanation of your oily scalp causes and recommended solutions, and (5) your first treatment based on the recommended solutions. The entire process takes about 60-90 minutes.

Will my oily scalp come back after treatment?

Our treatments target the root cause of excess oil production, not just the symptoms, which means results are long-lasting. However, factors like stress, diet, and hormones can influence scalp oil levels. We provide maintenance protocols and at-home care recommendations to help you maintain a balanced scalp long-term.

Should I wash my hair before my appointment?

Yes, arrive with clean, dry hair free of styling products. However, don't over-wash - your normal washing routine is fine. This ensures our AI analysis can accurately assess your scalp's natural oil production and treatments can penetrate effectively.

Can I still use my regular hair products during treatment?

We'll recommend specific products suited to your scalp condition. Heavy styling products and silicone-based conditioners may need to be avoided as they can clog follicles. Your consultant will provide a personalised product guide during your consultation.
